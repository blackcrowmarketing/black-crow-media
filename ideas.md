# Black Crow Media - Design Brainstorming

## 1. The "Brutalist Terminal" Aesthetic
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalism / Cyberpunk
**Core Principles:** Raw, functional, developer-focused, high-contrast.
**Color Philosophy:** Monochromatic (Black/White) with a single electric accent (Neon Green or Cyber Yellow). Represents the "code-first" nature of the Agency OS.
**Layout Paradigm:** Grid-heavy, visible borders, modular "cards" that look like terminal windows.
**Signature Elements:** Monospace typography, blinking cursors, raw HTML elements styled as UI.
**Interaction Philosophy:** Instant feedback, keyboard-driven navigation hints, "command line" style inputs.
**Animation:** Glitch effects on hover, typewriter text reveal, sharp transitions (no ease-in-out).
**Typography System:** JetBrains Mono (Headings & Body) - Pure code aesthetic.
</text>

</response>

## 2. The "Swiss International" Aesthetic
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Swiss Style (International Typographic Style)
**Core Principles:** Clarity, order, asymmetry, mathematical precision.
**Color Philosophy:** Stark white background, deep charcoal text, and a bold "Black Crow" red accent. Communicates authority and precision.
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric grid, ample whitespace, strong vertical axis.
**Signature Elements:** Large typography, geometric shapes, high-quality photography of "clean workspaces".
**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, precise, "premium" feel.
**Animation:** Subtle parallax, smooth reveals, "magnetic" buttons.
**Typography System:** Helvetica Now (or Inter) - Bold, tight tracking for headings; clean, legible body.
</text>
<probability>0.05</probability>
</response>

## 3. The "Dark Mode SaaS" Aesthetic
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Modern SaaS / Linear-style
**Core Principles:** Sleek, depth, immersion, focus.
**Color Philosophy:** Deep slate/black background, subtle gradients (purple/blue/teal), glowing borders. Represents "modern tech" and "future".
**Layout Paradigm:** Centralized content with "bento box" feature grids.
**Signature Elements:** Glassmorphism, subtle glows, rounded corners, gradient text.
**Interaction Philosophy:** Delightful micro-interactions, "bouncy" feedback.
**Animation:** Smooth fades, glowing trails, floating elements.
**Typography System:** Inter (Headings & Body) - The standard for modern tech legibility.
</text>
